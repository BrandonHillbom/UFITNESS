UFITNESS

Welcome to UFitness!


UFITNESS is an application specifically designed to provide resources and aid to people wanting to make a change in their lives. One of the most difficult aspects of beginning your fitness journey is finding what types of exercises and nutritional plan will get you seeing results relatively quic. Many people tend to stick to their fitness goals for short periods of time because of this reason. Luckily, the UFITNESS experience will motivate you to stay on track while giving high quality resources that can get you reaching your goals while not suffering with unpleasant diets. This applications's most notable features can be viewed below.

Body Type Quiz

Everyone's body's are different and require different training and nutrition methods to achieve their goals. Most people fit into one of three body types: ectomorph, mesomorph, and endomorph. By identifying which of these three types you are, in combination with other factors such as your goals, eating habits, and desire to train through our body type quiz, you will be one step closer to realizing your potential!

Fitness Tracker

One of the most challenging aspects to attaining one's fitness goals is sticking to a program until until the end. We at UFitness completely understand the dissapointment of not seeing your progress. However, we tend to only focus on macroscopic properties rather than data that shows undeniable progress. Through our fitness tracker, you will be able to see your improvement by tracking calorie and protein intake, weight loss/gain, muscle growth, strength, and endurance! You can also select foods that you eat throughout the day and we will automatically calculate nutritional information for you!

Nutrition Guide

Nothing is worse when trying to physically and mentally better yourself than when you research what you should be eating and instead, you are given general servings or meals that even Gordon Ramsey would struggle to cook! Our nutrition guide provides you with meals and supplemental snacks that combine convenience, health, and taste to give you exactly what you are looking for! There are also extensive resources and information to help you create your own versions of these meals to ensure the nutritional plan is working for you instead of you working for it.

Exercise Guide

Everyone has different experiences with weightlifting and exercising so while many people may know exactly what workouts they should be doing, others may be on the ground floor. This section features muscle group targeting exercises as well as step by step instruction on how to perform each movement. Additionally, information is given on what people tend to do wrong and tips to improve your form, thus achieving optimal growth.
